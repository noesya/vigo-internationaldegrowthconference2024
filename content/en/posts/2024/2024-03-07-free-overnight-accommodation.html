Pontevedra City Council has provided us with a covered space close to the conference venue. Participants can stay there free of charge during the conference week.

Participants must register in advance to request this service via this form and must bring their own sleeping material (mat and sleeping bag). The site has toilets and washbasins, but the showers are located elsewhere (nearby) and have limited use.

The site is guarded by a security service with access control. A cash deposit of 50€ is required at the entrance as a guarantee of correct use of the site and compliance with the rules. This deposit will be refunded at the end of the stay.

Información útil:

  • Where: Recinto Ferial - Pazo da Cultura
    Location: https://maps.app.goo.gl/foq3xYZu3Xyvj6BQ9
  • Number of places: 100
  • Dates: From  16 to  23 June. 
  • Showers: Pavillón Universitario de Pontevedra (de luns a venres de 9 da mañá a 2 do mediodía). https://maps.app.goo.gl/7oaedUmteWWPjDeBA
