The Conference will count on two big types of sessions: 

  • Conventional sessions: related to topics proposed by the Organization and the Scientific Committee. The works to be presented within these sessions will be selected by the reviewers designated by the Organizing Committee.
  • Special sessions: selected among the proposals received during the “call for special session proposals” period, that will open in September. Applicants will propose the topics and also the modality of the session, and if their proposals are selected, they will be in charge of organizing their sessions.

The sessions will be developed under different modalities:

  • Parallel sessions
  • Plenaries
  • Interactive sessions
  • Artistic sessions
  • Posters

Conventional sessions will be organized in 1.5 hours slots (having up to 12 parallel sessions per slot). Each session will count on a chair assigned by the Organization and the Scientific Committee, and the presentations will be about a common topic (there can be more than one session about the same topic during the Conference). The topics of the sessions will be those proposed by the Organization and the special topics selected after the “special sessions” call. 

There will be a maximum of 4 presentations per parallel session, and the participants will count on a maximum of 15 minutes for each presentation so that there can be enough time to have an enriching discussion.
