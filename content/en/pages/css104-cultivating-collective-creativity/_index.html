The evaluation will adopt a Māori epistemology for sharing knowledge. The goal of this epistemology is ensuring the flourishing of ecosystems. For flourishing to occur, the life supporting capacity of ecosystems (mauri) must be enhanced and understood from the perspective of intrinsic belonging.
