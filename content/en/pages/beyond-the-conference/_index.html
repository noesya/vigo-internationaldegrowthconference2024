The organization of a scientific event of this nature represents a unique opportunity to bring the public closer to science; to make known to society the work done by researchers in the fields of ecological economics and degrowth; to raise public awareness of the need to establish new ways of social development and to address the ecological transition; and to highlight the importance of society and scientific community to approach, understand each other and establish collaborations.

With these objectives in mind, Pontevedra Degrowth 2024 comprises different activities focused on the civil society, emphasizing young people.
