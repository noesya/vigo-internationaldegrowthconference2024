Alternatives are not easy to articulate or to harness into workable solutions because the neoliberal logic is highly resistant. Ironically, to counter the damages stemming from the thoroughgoing economic institutionalisation, which remains all but blind to the plural values of nature, more formalisation and economic valorisation is often prescribed.
