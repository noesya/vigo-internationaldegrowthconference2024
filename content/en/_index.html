The city of Pontevedra (Galicia, Spain) will host the 10th International Degrowth Conference and the 15th Conference of the European Society for Ecological Economics (ESEE 2024). It will take place within the framework of the “Pontevedra Degrowth 2024” year, which will turn Pontevedra into the world capital of Degrowth and Ecological Economics in 2024.

Under the title Technology, science, and innovation beyond growth: cultivating collective creativity for a sustainable future, this event will comprise two kinds of activities: 

  • The Conference itself, which will be held on June 18-21, 2024, and where is expected participation of more than 500 people. It will count on several sessions, including lectures, workshops, and artistic spaces.
  • Beyond The Conference activities, which will be citizen-oriented and developed in several city locations, counting on initiatives such as conferences, debates, exhibitions, or contests.

The event is being organized according to degrowth principles, such as cooperation, transparency, sustainability, democracy, involvement of a wide diversity of partners, inclusiveness, solidarity…

The Organizing Committee encourages everyone to join us in this event and stay tuned to our website and our social networks so as not to miss anything!

Although the event is promoted by these three entities, the organization follows a collaborative philosophy, being involved academics from many research centers and universities, local and regional administrations, activists, artists, local companies, citizens, and other actors.

The joint International Degrowth and European Society for Ecological Economics conference will be held in Pontevedra in June 2024. The Río Lérez, is a river that runs through the city and provides the inspiration for the conference's identity. It runs through the circle, connecting people to water and nature. The symbol represents a meeting point for different academic disciplines, students, and the public; a social site for important discussion and knowledge exchange.
