The city of Pontevedra (Galicia, Spain) will host in 2024 the 10th International Degrowth Conference and the 15th Conference of the European Society for Ecological Economics (ESEE 2024). It will take place within the framework of the “Pontevedra Degrowth 2024” year, which will turn Pontevedra into the world capital of degrowth and ecological economics during the next year.

Under the title Technology, science, and innovation beyond growth: Unleashing collective creativity for a sustainable future, this event will comprise two kinds of activities: 

  • The Conference itself, which will be held on June 18-21, 2024, and where is expected participation of more than 500 people. It will count on several sessions, including lectures, workshops, and artistic spaces.
  • The Beyond The Conference activities, that will be citizen-oriented and developed in several city locations, counting on initiatives such as conferences, debates, exhibitions, or contests.

The event is being organized according to degrowth principles, such as cooperation, transparency, sustainability, democracy, involvement of a wide diversity of partners, inclusiveness, solidarity…

The Organizing Committee encourages everyone to join us in this event. Stay tuned to our website and our social networks so as not to miss anything!
